CyJump is a Parameter Tampering challenge

Examine the URL -> host-wmmydsy0-prod.prod.cywar.xyz:32863/?jump=10&delay_between_packets=18
In the game, identify how the character moves via the jump and delay_between_packets parameters listed in the URL
Modify jump=10 to a value higher than 10. I entered jump=100
Modify delay_between_packets=18 to a value lower than 18. I entered delay_between
Proceed with your modifications to the URL to the end of the game to Capture the Flag
